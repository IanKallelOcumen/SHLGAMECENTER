## 🎯 **User Flow**

1. **Land on Page** → See hero with live match status
2. **Scroll Down** → Mini score bar appears at top
3. **Browse Tabs** → Navigate between Summary, Stats, Lineups, H2H
4. **Read News** → Auto-scrolling carousel with real articles
5. **Check Stats** → Interactive charts and comparisons
6. **View Lineups** → Toggle between list and ice formation
7. **Vote & Engage** → Match prediction, social feed
8. **Share Match** → Use native share functionality
9. **Preferences Saved** → Theme and language persist
